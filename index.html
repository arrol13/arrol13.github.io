About Me

I’m a graduate in Electrical and Electronics from St Joseph Engineering College, Mangalore. My courses in Electrical, Electronics, Automation and Control Engineering Technologies have equipped me with a solid base upon which I plan to build my career. I’m specialized in PLC-SCADA programming, Relay Logic Wiring and Design, Wiring and Design of Control Panels and Motor Control Centre (MCC) Panels, Variable Frequency Drives (VFD), Human Machine Interface (HMI), AutoCAD, Electrical Machines, Power Systems and Solar Systems.

I interned at Karnataka Power Corporation Ltd. (KPCL), Bangalore which focused on technology evaluation of solar energy, site selection, system design, wiring and electrical fundamentals, sizing of solar systems, installation, commissioning, operation and maintenance standards, safety in installation and commissioning and earthing of solar plants.

My academic experiences have greatly contributed to the development of my technical, teamwork, planning and time-management skills. I excel at communicating both clearly and professionally with co-workers, management and customers.
